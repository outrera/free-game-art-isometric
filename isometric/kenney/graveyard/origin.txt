This package includes over 50 different graveyard and Halloween themed models, including graves, coffins, fences, benches, characters and more.

Features:

    Works with Unity, Unreal and all other 3D engines (instructions)
    50+ objects!
    Optimized, low poly models

Files:

    Separate FBX, OBJ, DAE, GTLF and STL models
    Isometric renders (4 angles)
    Sideview renders
    Import instructions for the most popular game engines

Let me know if you have any questions or requests!
