Includes 3D models to build spaceports and other galactic structures, also includes isometric and top-down renders of each tile. Includes buildings, rocks, ores, spaceships, characters, weapons and more.

Features:

    Works in Unity, Unreal and all other 3D engines
    Over 75 objects/tiles!
    Optimized, low poly models
    Unity package included
    Also includes isometric (4 or 8 angles) and topdown renders

Files:

    Separate OBJ and FBX models/textures (PNG)
    Unity package
    Isometric renders (4 or 8 angles)
    Topdown renders
