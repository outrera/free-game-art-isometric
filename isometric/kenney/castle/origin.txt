This kit includes models (and isometric/top-down sprites) for building castles. Included are castle walls, towers, gates, siege weaponry, characters, flags and more.

Features:

    Works in Unity, Unreal and all other 3D engines
    60 objects!
    Optimized, low poly models
    Unity package included
    Also includes isometric (4 angles) and topdown renders

Files:

    Separate OBJ models and textures (PNG)
    Unity package
    Isometric renders (4 angles)
    Topdown renders

Let me know if you have any questions or requests!
