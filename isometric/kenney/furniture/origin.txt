This package includes 3D models and 2D isometric/top-down sprites of furniture objects and building materials. Includes chairs, sofas, tables, bookcases, kitchen, bathroom and more!

Features:

    Works in Unity, Unreal and all other 3D engines
    120 objects!
    Optimized, low poly models
    Unity package included

Files:

    Separate OBJ models and textures (PNG)
    Unity package
    Isometric renders (4 angles)
    Topdown renders

Let me know if you have any questions or requests!
