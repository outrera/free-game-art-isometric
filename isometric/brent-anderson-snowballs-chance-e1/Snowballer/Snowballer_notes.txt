Notes on the Snowballer files, for your ease of use.

Some of these sprites are pieces, combined in certain ways to show different action. Their draw order can be seen in their naming...

sballerL(legs)->sballerT(torso)->sballerH(head)

... any full body animation is assigned to the sballerL name.
